   About Us

ACET Computer, established in 2065BS, has been a trusted name in the field of computer technology and training.
Affiliated with the Council for Technical Education and Vocational Training (CTEVT), the institution has played a pivotal
role in empowering students with industry-relevant skills in computer hardware, software, and network maintenance.
With years of experience, ACET has become a hub for quality technical education, preparing students for dynamic roles
in the IT and technical sectors.

In addition to its training programs, ACET Computer is dedicated to creating job opportunities for its graduates by equipping
them with practical knowledge and certifications that meet industry standards. Many alumni have successfully secured
employment in IT companies, service centers, and other technical domains, while others have ventured into entrepreneurship,
opening their own repair and service businesses. The institution also operates as a retailer of computer peripherals, offering a
wide range of products such as laptops, desktops, keyboards, mice, printers, and accessories. ACET ensures high-quality and
affordable devices for students, professionals, and businesses alike. Their sales department provides expert guidance to customers,
helping them select devices tailored to their needs.

Moreover, ACET excels in repair and maintenance services for laptops, desktops, printers, and other devices. With a skilled team
of technicians, it delivers reliable solutions for both minor and complex issues, ensuring customer satisfaction. By integrating
education, retail, and technical services, ACET Computer continues to be a cornerstone in the field of computer technology.
